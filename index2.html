About Vitasage

Vitasage LLC FZ is a UAE-based life sciences and nutraceutical company established in the Meydan Free Zone, Dubai. The company was created with a clear mission: to develop and scale science-driven nutraceutical solutions that deliver real functional benefits, bridging academic research, advanced formulation, and global market execution.

Vitasage operates with an international mindset, focusing on innovation, regulatory readiness, and long-term partnerships. Rather than acting as a mass-market supplement brand, Vitasage positions itself as a platform for differentiated actives, novel formulations, and high-quality products designed for regulated and premium markets worldwide.

Science & R&D - Vitasage

Science and research are the foundation of everything developed by Vitasage. The company’s R&D strategy is centered on the identification of novel bioactive compounds, scientific validation of their mechanisms of action, and the translation of this knowledge into safe, effective, and scalable nutraceutical formulations.

Vitasage works in collaboration with academic institutions, research partners, and specialized manufacturers to ensure that each product is supported by evidence, robust development protocols, and strict quality standards. From raw material selection to pilot batches and scale-up, the focus remains on innovation, reproducibility, and long-term scientific credibility.

From science to products

Our products are born from scientific research and validated development. Each formulation reflects Vitasage's commitment to innovation, evidence-based design, and global scalability.

One example of this approach is Cubexa, a science-driven nutraceutical brand developed within the Vitasage platform.
