About Vitasage

Vitasage LLC FZ is a UAE-based life sciences and nutraceutical company established in the Meydan Free Zone, Dubai. The company was created with a clear mission: to develop and scale science-driven nutraceutical solutions that deliver real functional benefits, bridging academic research, advanced formulation, and global market execution.

Vitasage operates with an international mindset, focusing on innovation, regulatory readiness, and long-term partnerships. Rather than acting as a mass-market supplement brand, Vitasage positions itself as a platform for differentiated actives, novel formulations, and high-quality products designed for regulated and premium markets worldwide.
